    Frequently Asked Questions

    1. How qualified are your teachers?

    Our teachers are highly qualified, with many years of experience teaching Chinese as a second language. They hold relevant teaching certifications and are skilled in creating personalized lessons to suit each student’s learning needs.

    2. How long is each class, and how many classes in one session?

    Each class is 50 minutes long. Typically, one session consists of two lessons, but we can adjust the schedule based on the student’s needs.

    3. What materials or resources will be used during the class?

    We use the most authoritative HSK official textbooks and resources. Additionally, we adjust the materials based on each student’s specific needs to ensure the most effective learning experience.

    4. How does the school confirm my Chinese level?

    Our teachers have extensive experience and will quickly assess your level through communication and instruction during the first lesson. This helps us understand your proficiency and tailor the lessons accordingly.

     

    5. Can I bring my own materials, and will the teacher help me prepare for learning?

    Yes, you can bring your own materials, and the teacher will be happy to incorporate them into your lessons. They will also provide guidance and additional resources to help you prepare and succeed.

    6. When can I start my classes?

    You can start your classes as soon as we finalize your enrollment and schedule. We offer flexible start dates based on your availability.
